## Decision made for tomorrow

**Nebius provider swap: Nebius AI Studio → SambaNova**, same model (Llama 3.3-70B).

Rationale:
- SambaNova delivers ~285 t/s output speed with sub-1s TTFT (vs Nebius Base currently 3–9s warm)
- Free tier: 240 RPM, 48,000 requests/day, no credit card required
- OpenAI-compatible API with bearer auth — drop-in replacement for existing router
- Model ID: `Meta-Llama-3.3-70B-Instruct`, endpoint: `https://api.sambanova.ai/v1`
- Cost: $0.60/M input, $1.20/M output. ~$0.30–0.50/month at 500 qpm demo volume, still under $1/M blended
- Groq considered and rejected: 12K TPM free tier cap would break demo after ~11 queries/min at our ~1,050 tokens/query average

Tomorrow opens with the SambaNova implementation prompt, not more research.
